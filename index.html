Overview

Seven Seasons, Ten Tablescapes

A two day, on site shoot at Vicky Bakery's BOH office, capturing styled catering and tablescape photography across seven seasonal programs, from Spring/Easter brunch through New Year's Eve. Built from the shot list and mood board Vicky Bakery provided on July 23.

Vicky's shot list groups everything under seven seasonal themes, but a few of those themes cover more than one food layout. Fall and Christmas each carry multiple distinct spreads, so the real production count is ten tablescape builds and 38 final images, the number the schedule and pricing below are built around.

Production plan

Two Days, Five Builds a Day

Ten full resets in one day is not a realistic ask alongside careful styling and multiple angles per build. Two days keeps the pace to five builds a day, which is workable if food and props are staged ahead of each call time.

Before we lock dates

Four Things to Confirm

  1. Will Vicky Bakery provide tablescape props, linens, and seasonal decor, or should BLANK plan and coordinate them? This is the difference between the core package and the styling add on.
  2. Is video coverage of interest alongside the photography?
  3. For "closeups of different sections of table" on each build, is one representative image enough, or would a small set of detail crops per build be more useful? This changes the final image count.
  4. Confirming intended usage, social, website, print menus, so final image specs and resolution are set correctly from day one.
